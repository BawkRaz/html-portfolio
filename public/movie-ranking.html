The Best Movies of All Time According to Daniel

My Top 4 Movies of All-Time.

Ghost in the Shell

The best cyberpunk film that influenced many other future films.

Akira

Established itself as a contender for amazing storytelling and animation.

Blade Runner

Science-fiction done in a grounding way with no aliens and more of a philosophy of what it means to be human.

Who Framed Roger Rabbit?

Best realtime and cartoon rendering for any film.
